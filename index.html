Our

Online yoga classes

Along with indoor exercising in our local studios we have created several online courses for those who don’t have enough time or resources to attend group lessons physically.

Power yoga

Power Yoga has been gaining popularity since 1990s and nowadays it’s hardly possible to find a person who didn’t hear about it. The concept of Power Yoga stems from Ashtanga and it implies moving really fast to music.

Meditation

There are a wide range of meditation practices among which you can find exactly what is suitable for you. All the techniques are based on the notion “mindfulness” that refers to any practice when you concentrate on your experiencing the moment.

Yoga for children

Yoga is not only for adults, it can be also helpful for children as it enhances their state of health, physical and mental strength. In our studios you can start practicing together with your children, because there are lots of options for kid-friendly poses for your offsprings.
